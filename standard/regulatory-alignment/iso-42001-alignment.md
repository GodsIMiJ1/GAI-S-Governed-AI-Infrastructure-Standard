# ISO/IEC 42001 Alignment

**Document ID:** GAIS-REG-002  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Informative Mapping

## 1. Purpose

This document maps GAI-S controls to ISO/IEC 42001 AI management system themes for implementation planning and audit evidence structuring.

## 2. Mapping

| ISO/IEC 42001 Theme | Relevant GAI-S Controls | Expected Evidence |
|---|---|---|
| Leadership, roles, accountability | `GAIS-CTRL-AS-01`, `GAIS-CTRL-AS-02` | Authority model, decision logs |
| Planning and risk treatment | `GAIS-CTRL-RC-01`, `GAIS-CTRL-RC-02` | CRI method, risk acceptance criteria |
| Operational controls | `GAIS-CTRL-DC-01`, `GAIS-CTRL-DC-02`, `GAIS-CTRL-DG-02` | Containment architecture, access enforcement |
| Monitoring and performance evaluation | `GAIS-CTRL-AL-01`, `GAIS-CTRL-AL-02` | Audit logs, retention evidence |
| Improvement and corrective action | `GAIS-CTRL-IR-01`, `GAIS-CTRL-IR-02` | Incident records, CAPA trackers |
| Data governance | `GAIS-CTRL-DG-01` | Provenance and quality documentation |

## 3. Notes

- GAI-S can be used as a technical control layer within an ISO/IEC 42001 management system.
- Organizations SHOULD maintain internal mapping to clause-level obligations relevant to scope.
